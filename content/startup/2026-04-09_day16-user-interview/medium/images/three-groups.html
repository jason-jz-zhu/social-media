User Interview Framework
Three Groups, Three Completely Different Signals
1
Current Users
Already using your product
Know existing pain points. Tell you what's broken, frustrating, needs polish. Pull toward incremental improvement.
Signal Type
Iteration feedback
2
Competitor Users
Using a rival product instead
Reveal strategic gaps: why they chose someone else. Twitch's competitor users wanted money, stability, global reach.
Signal Type
Strategic direction
3
Non-Users
Not in the market at all
Reveal market expansion blockers. Barriers may be technological, psychological, or structural.
Signal Type
Market size potential
Most teams only talk to Group 1 — the easiest to find, not the most useful
