Gottman's Framework Applied to Products
The Four Horsemen of Product Death
#1
Criticism
Blaming users for not understanding your product
"The user is wrong"
#2
Contempt
Treating user feedback with disdain or dismissal
"Users don't know what they want"
#3
Defensiveness
Responding to complaints with excuses
"It's not a bug, it's a feature"
Deadliest
#4
Stonewalling
Not responding to users at all
Silence kills products the same way it kills marriages
Stonewalling is the #1 predictor of early churn — not bugs, not bad design.
