Kevin Hale's Metaphor
Your Product Has Two Relationships
Where most teams focus
Dating
New User Acquisition
  • 1 Polished landing pages
  • 2 Beautiful onboarding
  • 3 First impressions matter
  • 4 Best behavior, best self
  • 5 One-time effort
VS
Where growth actually lives
Marriage
Existing User Retention
  • 1 Constant energy input
  • 2 Fast support responses
  • 3 Every interaction matters
  • 4 Requires real investment
  • 5 Compounds over time
Most companies put their A-team on dating and their B-team on the marriage.
