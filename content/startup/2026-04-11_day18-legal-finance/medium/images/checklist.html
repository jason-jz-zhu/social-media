Startup Operations
Legal & Finance Checklist for Founders
Formation & Equity
Incorporate in Delaware (C-Corp)
Standard structure every investor expects
Wrong state = $500K+ conversion cost
Split equity roughly equally
Ideas = $0. Execution is everything.
4-year vesting + 1-year cliff
Even solo founders. Signals commitment.
File 83(b) within 30 days
No extensions. No exceptions.
Deals blow up over this single form
Operations & Hygiene
Pay founders minimum wage
Unpaid wages = legal leverage for fired cofounders
Treat investor money as trust
"Would you be embarrassed?" test
Fire fast, cut access immediately
Face-to-face, third party present, revoke same day
GitHub hostage story: revoke access NOW
Keep all documents organized & signed
Missing docs kill deals during due diligence
