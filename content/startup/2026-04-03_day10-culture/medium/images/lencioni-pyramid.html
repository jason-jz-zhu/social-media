Lencioni's Framework
The Pyramid of Team Dysfunction
1
Trust (Foundation)
Vulnerability-based trust. Safe to admit mistakes.
2
Healthy Conflict
Honest debate, not artificial harmony.
3
Commitment
Buy-in from real discussion, not forced consensus.
4
Accountability
Peers hold each other to high standards.
5
Results
Collective outcomes over individual ego.
You can't skip levels. Build from the bottom up.
