Operations Framework
Barrels vs. Ammunition
Barrels
Rare — Limits Velocity
  • Concept to shipped product
  • Crosses all functional boundaries
  • Self-driven, no pushing needed
  • Expand scope until it breaks
Ammunition
Most People — Needs Direction
  • Talented and hardworking
  • Great at execution when aimed
  • Needs a barrel to drive output
  • More headcount ≠ more output
Company Velocity = Number of Barrels
