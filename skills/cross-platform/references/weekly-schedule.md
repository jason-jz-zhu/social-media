# Weekly Publishing Rhythm

| Day | Platform | Content | Source |
|-----|----------|---------|--------|
| Wed | — | Write Substack mother content | Original |
| Thu | Substack | Send newsletter | Mother content |
| Fri | Medium | Publish article | Derived from mother |
| Sat | — | Rest or prep XHS slides | — |
| Mon | XHS | Publish carousel | Chinese rewrite |
| Tue | LinkedIn | Publish post | Extracted insight |

## Notes

- **Wednesday** is the writing day. Focus entirely on producing the Substack newsletter as the "mother content." The outline (`shared/outline.md`) should be created first, then the full newsletter.
- **Thursday** is send day. Newsletter goes out to Substack subscribers. This gives you a full day between writing and sending for editing.
- **Friday** is Medium day. Derive the Medium article from the mother content using the `medium` skill. Optimize the title for SEO.
- **Saturday** is a rest/prep day. If XHS slides need extra polish or the topic requires visual assets, use this day.
- **Monday** is XHS day. Publish the Chinese carousel post. Best posting time: 18:30-20:00 Beijing time (or local evening for your audience).
- **Tuesday** is LinkedIn day. Publish the professional insight post. Best posting time: 8:00-9:00 AM local time (morning commute scroll).

## YouTube Cadence

YouTube scripts follow a separate, less frequent cadence (biweekly or monthly) since video production requires more effort. YouTube scripts can be drafted during the Wednesday writing session but don't need to publish on a fixed weekly day.
